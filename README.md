## What is FuelFare: Trip Cost Calculator?

**FuelFare: Trip Cost Calculator** is a free trip cost calculator app by [lapnito.cz s.r.o.](https://lapnito.cz) that estimates fuel expenses and driving costs for any trip. Enter your distance, fuel consumption, and fuel price, and FuelFare instantly calculates the total cost. Split the bill between passengers, add extra costs like tolls and parking, and save vehicles and routes for repeated use.

Available as **FuelFare: Trip Cost Calculator** on [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.tomas.fuelfare) and on the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/fuelfare-trip-cost-calculator/id6450358923).

Whether you're planning a road trip, estimating daily commute expenses, splitting gas money with carpool partners, or budgeting for a long-distance drive — FuelFare gives you a clear, transparent cost breakdown in seconds. The app works entirely offline, collects zero user data, and has no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.

## Key Features

- **Instant Trip Cost Estimate** — Enter distance, fuel consumption (L/100km or MPG), and fuel price to get an accurate trip cost in seconds.

## How Trip Cost Calculation Works

FuelFare uses a straightforward formula to calculate trip fuel cost:

**Fuel Cost = (Distance ÷ 100) × Fuel Consumption × Fuel Price**

For example, a 250 km trip with a car that consumes 7.5 L/100km at a fuel price of $1.50/L:

```
Fuel needed = 250 ÷ 100 × 7.5 = 18.75 liters
Fuel cost   = 18.75 × $1.50 = $28.13
```

If you add $5.00 in tolls and $3.00 in parking, the total trip cost becomes $36.13. Split between 3 passengers, each person pays $12.04.

The "Show Math" feature displays this entire breakdown step by step, so you always know how the final number was calculated. FuelFare supports both metric (L/100km, km) and imperial (MPG, miles) units.

**How accurate is FuelFare?** The calculation is mathematically precise based on the inputs you provide. Real-world costs may vary slightly due to driving style, traffic, terrain, and fuel price fluctuations. For the most accurate results, use your car's actual measured fuel consumption rather than the manufacturer's rated figure.

**Does it account for electric vehicles?** FuelFare is designed for combustion engine vehicles using fuel consumption in L/100km or MPG. For electric vehicles, you would need to convert your energy consumption (kWh/100km) to an equivalent "fuel" figure.

## FAQ

**Q: Is FuelFare free?**
A: Yes, FuelFare is completely free on both Android and iOS. There are no ads, no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no account required.

**Q: Does it work without internet?**
A: Yes, FuelFare works completely offline. All calculations, saved vehicles, routes, and trip history are stored locally on your device. No internet connection is needed.

**Q: How do I split gas costs with passengers?**
A: Enter your trip details as usual, then set the number of passengers. FuelFare divides the total trip cost (fuel plus any extras like tolls) evenly and shows each person's share.

**Q: Can I save my car's fuel consumption?**
A: Yes, you can save multiple vehicle profiles with their fuel consumption and preferred fuel price. Set one as your default vehicle so it's automatically selected for new calculations.

**Q: What units does FuelFare support?**
A: FuelFare supports both metric (kilometers, liters, L/100km) and imperial (miles, gallons, MPG) units. You can switch between them in the app settings.

**Q: Does FuelFare collect my data?**
A: No. FuelFare does not collect any user data. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no network requests. Everything stays on your device.

**Q: Can I back up my data?**
A: Yes, you can export all your vehicles, routes, and trip history as a ZIP file using the Export feature. Import it on a new device or keep it as a backup.

**Q: What is the "Show Math" feature?**
A: "Show Math" displays the complete calculation breakdown for any trip — showing every step from distance and fuel consumption to the final cost. It helps you verify the result and understand exactly how the total was calculated.

**Q: Which devices are supported?**
A: Android 5.0 (Lollipop) and above, and iOS 17.0 and above. The app also runs on iPad.

**Q: What's the difference between the Android and iOS versions?**
A: Both versions offer the same core functionality. The app is called "FuelFare: Trip Cost Calculator" on both platforms.
